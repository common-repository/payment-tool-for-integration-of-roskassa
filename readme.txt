Payment Processing for Social Media Networks

== Description ==

Our company is a robust payment gateway that can power any payment processing needs for your social media platform. Roskassa Payment Gateway offers a secure, user-friendly and seamless platform for collecting online payments in real-time and getting a digital platform to accept the payments online can ultimately improve the overall performance of your business. Roskassa's full-featured solution integrates state-of-the art security features to protect you and your user base. With solutions like membership and subscription pricing, let Roskassa take your social network viral while keeping your payments simple and centralized.

Roskassa payment aggregator supports payment methods:

*   Bank cards
*   YuMoney
*   QIWI
*   WebMoney
*   Perfect money
*   Payeer
*   Bitcoin
*   Litecoin
*   Ethereum
*   Advcash
*   PayPal
*   Apple Pay
